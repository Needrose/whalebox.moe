Report Abuse

If there is a file hosted on Whalebox that violates our Terms of Service or is your own copyrighted content, please report it to us by emailing absue@whalebox.moe with the following information:

1. The URL of the file.
2. Why the file violates our Terms of Service or proof of your ownership of the copyrighted content.
3. A request to remove the file.

We will review your report as soon as possible and if we verify that the file violates our Terms of Service or is copyright, it will be removed and the uploader may get banned (depending on the severity of the violation).
